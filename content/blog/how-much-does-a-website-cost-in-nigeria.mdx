# How Much Does a Website Cost in Nigeria?

For Nigerian business owners, the question "How much does a website cost?" is often met with the frustrating answer: "It depends.". In 2026, the digital landscape in Nigeria has matured, and pricing has become more segmented based on functionality, design complexity, and long-term business goals..

Whether you are a startup in Lagos looking for a simple landing page or an e-commerce brand in Abuja scaling nationwide, this guide breaks down the current market rates for web design, hosting, and maintenance..

---

## 1. Website Design Costs by Project Type
The primary driver of your initial investment is the type of website you need.. Here is the current 2026 pricing breakdown for development:

| Website Type | Estimated Price Range (₦) | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Landing Page** | ₦80,000 – ₦210,000 | Single products or lead generation |
| **Basic Business Site** | ₦150,000 – ₦450,000 | Small businesses and startups |
| **Standard Corporate** | ₦450,000 – ₦1,200,000 | Growing SMEs and professional services |
| **E-Commerce Store** | ₦500,000 – ₦2,500,000+ | Selling products with payment integration |
| **Custom Web App** | ₦1,500,000 – ₦15,000,000+ | SaaS and complex fintech platforms |

---

## 2. Mandatory Recurring Costs (Domain & Hosting)
Beyond the design fee, every website requires a "home" (hosting) and an "address" (domain).. Many Nigerian providers now offer discounted bundles to help businesses get started..

### Domain Name Registration (Annual)
* **.com.ng**: ₦5,500 – ₦7,500 (Best for local trust and affordability).
* **.com**: ₦12,000 – ₦18,000.
* **.ng**: ₦15,000 – ₦17,500.

### Web Hosting (Monthly/Annual)
* **Starter Shared Hosting**: ₦1,200 – ₦2,500 per month (Ideal for blogs).
* **Business Hosting**: ₦6,000 – ₦9,000 per month (Recommended for high-traffic sites).
* **E-commerce/VPS Hosting**: ₦10,000+ per month (Necessary for speed and security).

---

## 3. Website Maintenance & SEO
A website is not a "set it and forget it" asset.. To stay secure and rank on search engines, you must budget for ongoing support..

* **Maintenance Plans**: Basic security updates range from **₦25,000 to ₦150,000 per year** for small sites..
* **SEO Services**: Monthly SEO packages to help your site appear on Google typically start at **₦50,000 to ₦150,000** for freelancers..

---

## 4. Why Do Prices Vary So Much?
You may receive quotes for the "same" project ranging from ₦100k to ₦1M.. This disparity is usually due to:
* **Custom vs. Template**: Template sites are cheaper but less flexible; custom sites offer better performance..
* **Functionality**: Features like Paystack/Flutterwave integration or bilingual content add development time..
* **Content Creation**: Professional copywriting and SEO-optimized images are often billed separately..

> **SEO Pro Tip**: Don't just look at the launch price. A cheap website that doesn't rank or load quickly will cost you more in lost leads than a professional build..
